Welcome to Table Banking

A community-based savings and lending strategy that enables individuals, especially those in low-income or underserved communities, to come together and pool their financial resources for mutual benefit.

How Table Banking works

Empowering women through financial inclusion.

What is Table Banking?

Table Banking is a group-based funding system where members of a group meet weekly and make weekly savings to form a kitty from which members can borrow. Each member’s contributions are recorded in her passbook and referred to as her share. The minimum contribution per week, which can be as low as Ksh 25 (20p), is set in the group’s constitution, which is written by the group itself. Thus it is set with the members’
